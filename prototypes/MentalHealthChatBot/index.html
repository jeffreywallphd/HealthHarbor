Why is mental health important ?

Overall Health

Mental and physical health are closely connected. Mental health issues can increase the risk for physical health problems and vice versa.

Productivity and Performance

Mental wellness helps improve productivity and performance in various aspects of life, including work, school, and sports.

Relationships

It plays a significant role in the health of our relationships. Good mental health allows us to make meaningful connections with others and to contribute positively to our communities.

Quality of Life

Mental wellness enhances our quality of life, increases our capacity for joy, creativity, and fulfillment, and enables us to enjoy life and cope with its challenges.
